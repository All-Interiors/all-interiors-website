WINDOW TREATMENTS

CURTAINS / DRAPERY
Our curtain workshop manufactures custom made curtains from the classics to the very latest trends.
All Interiors will supply a wide selection of decorative choices in curtain hardware, curtain tracks, poles, finials, brackets, hold backs / tie backs, etc. to complete a well dressed window.

Our curtain workshop is also equipped to manufacture heavy duty stage and conference curtains.

Tie back curtain

All Interiors also offers fully automated motorized electrical systems with remote controls to suit the curtain application.  Our team of experienced installers will install the motorized system with confidence and skill.

BLINDS
All styles of blinds are catered for including venetian blinds in wood or aluminum, roller blinds, woven bamboo blinds, woven wood roll up blinds, vertical blinds, roman blinds, festoon & unstructured roman blinds & sliding blind panels. Hardware available to consider; Roman Blind Head rails, battery operated roll up blinds or motorized systems with remote controls.

All window treatments are personally measured on site to assure a perfect fit & finish.

SPACE PLANING

Love the grand ideas and furniture, but not sure if it will fit or suit the interior space?

A colour board will broadcast a preview of the design intent with fabrics, wall and floor finishes, lighting, fittings, window treatments and furniture placement.
