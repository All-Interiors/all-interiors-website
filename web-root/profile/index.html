Profile

Glenda realized her passion for beautiful interiors by following a career commanded by this passion. She successfully obtained a qualification in Interior Design and Interior Décor from Dorothy van t’Riet Design Academy in Johannesburg.

Hard work and dedication gained her invaluable experience by working within the industry from the ground up, giving her the tools of understanding the fundamentals of curtain making, soft furnishing and furniture manufacture as well as procurement and project management.

In 2006 All Interiors and Hospitality Projects was founded. Since then numerous successful projects have been realized due to a combination of dedication, honesty, reliability and product knowledge with a commitment to follow through on the client’s brief and vision.

Her unique flair for interiors forged with a practical sense has been key to many beautiful spaces created.

All projects are undertaken professionally and are personally supervised from inception to handover.
